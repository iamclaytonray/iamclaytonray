## 🚀 Products

### [withcoach.me](https://withcoach.me)
withcoach.me is the AirBnB of private+group coaching across Southeast Asia (where I live!). 
- **Discovery**: SEA often operates in backchannels, across LINE Groups mostly. WithCoach enables fast discovery + quick comparisons. Find a coach you actually love in minutes.
- **Digital Payments**: SEA also operates primarily on cash or local payment rails (ie: PromptPay). We enable digital payments, including standard debit/credit cards. Now, tourists and expats can find a coach whenever they want to.
- **Realtime Translations**: Don't speak Thai, Vietnamese, Mandarin, etc? No problem. Text in your language and WithCoach handles automatic translations in real time. No Google Translate needed! And for locals? The website + mobile app offers translations in several major languages across Asia.
- **Coaching Infrastructure**: Coaches are our users too! Coaches have all of the infrastructure they need for managing their coaching business, without the admin.

### [Raft](https://tryotterhalf.com)
We believe community is everything.
